About Our Atelier

Buenos Aires, Argentina

Yaelle is a brand that started off as a teenage dream. After many years studying arts and then fashion design, we began projecting it in the comfort of our room. Starting with different ideas such as embroidery designs, day-to-day clothes, recycled gowns, et cetera.

Once the final idea was decided we started designing, cutting and sewing. And then the brand took off.

Nowadays, is a well known brand with more than 1200 employees from textile designers, pattern makers, embroiderer, illustrators, marketing and PR, retail, and so many.
